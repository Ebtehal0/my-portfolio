project info
Jun-Aug/2021 (3M)

UX research and UI design to help user to find the best recipes for drinks (coffee or tea). By using figma , zeplin and Miro.

Try the prototype

Discovery

Research & Analysis

In this step, I wanted to have a deep understanding of my users, what is their wants and needs, is there any features they are interested in ? are they want an easy way to prepare recipes or do they want to make a professional on, I wanted to know their background of types of coffee and tea and more questions like that. So, after I conducted the interviews and after analyzed data I collected from questioners I found a lot of ideas and insights that helped me a lot in designing my app.

Design

Concepts & Sketching

After I done my research and analysis data, I started to apply these insights and ideas into sketches then I used the research synthesis to decide the most important feature for the users to apply in the app and I used crazy-8 technique to sketch 8 features and ideas to implement but I choose the best 3 of them and implemented them on the low-fidelity prototype.

Develop

Prototyping

I started to apply the 3 features that I choose earlier, and I designed a low fidelity prototype then I tested it by users and took their notes then iterated the design depends on the user's feedbacks.

Test

Validation, Usability, Feedback

Low fidelity Prototype
  • Users found it confusing to choose the type of coffee or tea first.
  • Adding a welcome page clarifies the app idea easily.
  • users found the "let is go" button looks like the "sign-in" button and they thought there is a sign-in process.
High fidelity Prototype
  • 30% of the users could not go back to the main page.
  • User took about 10s- 15s to press view button.
  • 40% of the users were upset because there is no scroll in the prototype.

Design

Iteration

I iterated my app UI designs based on Data & KPIs that took from usability test and user's feedbacks and I also changed some of the text colors to comply with accessibility standards.

Solution

After taken participants' notes and feedbacks about the previous prototype, I Iterated the prototype, and it will reduce the time the users spent on finishing the flow and reduce dropping off percent in the Recipe page in the app.
